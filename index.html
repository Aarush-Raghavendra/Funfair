How it all works



So when you come in to the funfair (Ashburton Park) you have to go to the ticket booth.
They will give you the price for bundles of tickets and after you go buy tickets you can enjoy the rides we have to offer.
If you are buying something from the snacks or food stall then you have to pay with money or card.

Rules

Rides on demand




The Wagon Wheel

The Wagon Wheel also known as the ride of the heros. Is one of the most fastest rides in this funfair. As you can see and hopefully imagine. This ride is very fast paced. Unlike the twister this ride has the potential to make you vomit. A staff will be next to a dispenser which lets you get a free vomit bag.



Maximum Capaciy Per Seat: 150kg

Length of ride (Time): 10 mins

Price: £10 or 1 prebooked ticket




The Twister

The twister is a very enjoyable ride. It is reccomended to people who cant take extreme rides but don't like simple rides. This ride is for people who want to have the thrill of moving rapidly fast in the air but also not feeling dizzy after. This ride is very popular so if tickets online run out please go to the ticket booth and reserve you sport.
High Demands



Maximum Capaciy Per Seat: 150kg

Length of ride (Time): 5 mins

Price: £5 or 1 tickets
